Descriptions of each session offered can be found on the Sessions page. Please do not register for more than one session a week. Sessions that are offered multiple times will cover the same material. All sessions are in-person on Paly Campus

After receiving email notification that you are off the wait list, payment within 48 hrs will be required. If payment is not received your spot will cancelled.

Please confirm that you will pay the fee ($475 per session per camper) via the PayPal button on the next page. If you signed up for a waitlisting session, then you do not need to pay the fee for that session until receiving a spot.

If you are unable to pay through PayPal or with a credit/debit card via PayPal, please finish registering and contact us via email. If you need financial aid, please also finish registering and contact us by email.

Thank you for signing up. Please pay the camp fee via the PayPal button below. Simply donate the owed amount, which is $475 for one session and $950 for two sessions. You can pay directly through PayPal or with a credit/debit card via PayPal. Please write your students name in the PayPal form for easier processing.

Payment

We accept payment through PayPal or credit/debit card via PayPal. If you are unable to pay using either of these options, please contact us at summercamp@palyrobotics.com. Please pay as soon as you register using the PayPal button on the confirmation page. All camp registrations that do not have payment accounted for within 48 hours of signing up will be canceled.

Cancellations and Refunds

As we understand that plans often change over the summer, we will refund any registration cancellations before April 9 with a processing fee of $12.05 per 1 week of camp payment. Any cancellations made after April 9th will incur a fee of $200 per session. However, if the situation arises in which you cancel after May 7th, we unfortunately will not be able to refund you.

Note

Please make sure to use the same email as the provided parent email for the PayPal

Thank you for your interest in the camp. An automated confirmation email should be sent shortly. Please contact us if you never get this email.

If you have any questions, feel free to check out the FAQ page or contact us at summercamp@palyrobotics.com.
